# Boron Sources

## Knowledge

- [NIH Office of Dietary Supplements: Boron Fact Sheet for Health Professionals](https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Boron-HealthProfessional/)
  Best starting source for intake, supplement forms, food sources, health areas, safety, and the US adult tolerable upper intake level. Use for grounding any general claim.
- [National Academies / NCBI Bookshelf: Dietary Reference Intakes, boron UL table](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK208874/)
  Primary DRI reference table. Use when checking official US/Canada upper limits and the meaning of a UL.
- [EFSA Scientific Opinion: Tolerable Upper Intake Level of Boron](https://www.efsa.europa.eu/en/efsajournal/pub/80)
  European safety review for boric acid and borates. Use when comparing US and EU risk thresholds.
- [ATSDR / NCBI Bookshelf: Toxicological Profile for Boron, Health Effects](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK599075/)
  Toxicology-heavy source for high exposure, occupational exposure, developmental and reproductive endpoints, and uncertainty in the evidence base.
- [WHO: Chemical Fact Sheet - Boron in Drinking Water](https://www.who.int/publications/m/item/chemical-fact-sheets--boron)
  Use for environmental exposure and drinking-water context, not supplement efficacy.
- [Naghii et al. 2011: Comparative effects of daily and weekly boron supplementation on plasma steroid hormones and proinflammatory cytokines](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21129941/)
  Small human hormone/inflammation study often cited in testosterone claims. Use as a case study in promise plus limitations.
- [Ferrando and Green 1993: Boron supplementation in male bodybuilders](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/8508192/)
  Small resistance-training study that did not show meaningful effects on testosterone, lean mass, or strength. Use as the counterweight to testosterone-boosting claims.
- [ISSN Exercise and Sport Nutrition Review](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC2853497/)
  Sports-nutrition review that summarizes boron as a proposed testosterone/anabolism supplement with weak performance evidence.
- [Meacham et al. 1994: Boron supplementation and bone/mineral markers in female athletes](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/7889886/)
  Human bone/mineral study. Use when learning the difference between bone mineral density, mineral handling, and actual fracture outcomes.

## Communities

- [r/ScientificNutrition](https://www.reddit.com/r/ScientificNutrition/)
  Source-required nutrition discussion. Use only for finding papers and seeing critique, not for personal dosing decisions.
- Clinician or pharmacist with access to your medication list and labs
  Best real-world feedback source for whether boron supplementation fits your health context.

## Gaps

- Need better sources on long-term low-dose boron supplementation in healthy adults.
- Need better human data separating total testosterone, free testosterone, SHBG, estradiol, and symptoms over months rather than days.
- Need better fracture, bone mineral density, and osteoarthritis trials with clinically meaningful endpoints.
