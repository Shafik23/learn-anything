# Boron

Deep science learning workspace for boron supplementation, testosterone, bone health, mineral metabolism, inflammation, dosing, and safety.

## Mission

Build enough evidence-reading and physiology knowledge to judge boron claims about testosterone, SHBG, estradiol, bone health, mineral metabolism, inflammation, dosing, and safety without relying on supplement marketing.

Success looks like:

- Explain what boron does in food, supplements, and human physiology.
- Separate strong evidence, plausible mechanisms, weak evidence, and unsupported claims.
- Interpret boron-related hormone, bone, vitamin D, calcium, magnesium, and safety studies.
- Bring informed questions to a clinician or pharmacist when personal health context matters.

Constraints:

- Prefer deep science over quick supplement advice.
- Treat safety, dosing, lab interpretation, and medication context as health decisions, not casual biohacking.
- Use current high-trust sources and cite claims in lessons.
- Avoid borax ingestion protocols, megadosing, and cancer-treatment claims.

## Roadmap

- Evidence map: how to classify boron claims.
- Hormone model: total testosterone, free testosterone, SHBG, estradiol, and symptoms.
- Bone model: mineral handling, vitamin D, bone turnover, BMD, and fracture outcomes.
- Safety model: forms, dose ranges, upper limits, toxicity endpoints, and personal-context questions.
