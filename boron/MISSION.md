# Mission: Boron Deep Science

## Why
You already take 3 mg of supplemental boron and want to understand the compound well enough to judge claims about testosterone, SHBG, estradiol, bone health, mineral metabolism, inflammation, dosing, and safety without relying on supplement marketing.

## Success looks like
- Explain what boron does in food, supplements, and human physiology.
- Separate strong evidence, plausible mechanisms, weak evidence, and unsupported claims.
- Interpret boron-related hormone, bone, vitamin D, calcium, magnesium, and safety studies.
- Make informed questions for a clinician or pharmacist about your own 3 mg/day use.

## Constraints
- Prefer deep science over quick supplement advice.
- Treat safety, dosing, lab interpretation, and medication context as health decisions, not casual biohacking.
- Use current high-trust sources and cite claims in lessons.

## Out of scope
- Personalized medical dosing changes unless handled with a qualified clinician.
- Borax ingestion protocols, megadosing, or cancer-treatment claims.
